Spatio-Temporal Emissions Estimator for Passenger vehicles (STEEP)


Welcome! On this website you find the STEEP model developed by Team Inselberg. This project is part of the Academic Consultancy Training project at Wageningen University, in The Netherlands.

This project is commissioned by Tensing.

Background

As the largest source of CO2 emissions in Europe the transportation sector plays an important role in meeting the European Commission's environmental targets set out by the Paris Agreement. Of the total transportation emissions 70% come from road transit, thus a route planning model which can quantify the spatial and temporal distribution of emissions would be useful for both planners and commuters to evaluate their impact. As no other model capturing the time-sensitivity of carbon emissions exists our group created STEEP - Spatio-Temporal Emission Estimator for passenger vehicles.

The model considers vehicle characteristics and the driving environment based on time of departure. The result shows total emissions of a trip and indicates how congestion and free flow affect the overall emissions produced.
